Privacy Policy

Effective May 25, 2026

This policy explains what data the Boges2Birds mobile app collects, why it's collected, and how it's used. Boges2Birds is a personal golf training app — we collect only what's needed to make the app work and to improve it.

What we collect

When you create an account or use the app, we collect:

  • Account info — your email address and a display name you choose.
  • Practice data — your selected program (Break 100/90/80), skill self-ratings, session schedule, generated training blocks, and which drills you've completed.
  • Round logs — golf round stats you enter manually (score, fairways hit, greens in regulation, putts, penalties, course name, date played).
  • Subscription status — whether you have an active premium subscription, managed through Apple and RevenueCat.
  • Diagnostic data — anonymous crash reports and performance data to help us find and fix bugs.
  • Feedback you send us — if you submit feedback through the in-app form, we receive the text, your email, and basic device info you've consented to share.

We do not collect: your location, contacts, photos, microphone or camera data, advertising identifiers, or anything used for cross-app tracking.

How your data is stored

The app is offline-first. Your data is stored on your device in a local database, and a copy is synced in the background to our cloud provider so you can keep your account across devices. Nothing leaves your device until that sync runs.

Who processes your data

We use a small set of trusted service providers to run the app. Each one only sees the data needed for its specific purpose:

  • Supabase — stores your account, practice data, and round logs in a secure Postgres database.
  • Apple & RevenueCat — process subscription purchases and renewals. Apple handles all payment information; we never see your card details.
  • Sentry — receives anonymous crash and performance reports so we can fix bugs.
  • Resend — delivers in-app feedback submissions to the developer by email.

How your data is used

  • To run the app's core features (training blocks, drill checklists, round logging).
  • To personalize practice recommendations based on the stats you log.
  • To diagnose crashes and improve reliability.
  • To respond to support requests and feedback you send us.

We do not sell your data, share it with advertisers, or use it for advertising of any kind.

Your rights

You can:

  • Access your data — most of it is visible in the app's Profile and round log screens.
  • Update or correct your data — edit your profile, ratings, and logs at any time from inside the app.
  • Delete your account and all associated data — go to Profile → Delete Account in the app. Your data is removed from our servers immediately. If you need help, email us at the address below.
  • Cancel your subscription at any time through your Apple ID's subscription settings.

Children

Boges2Birds is intended for users 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has signed up, contact us and we'll remove the account.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we'll update the effective date above and post the new version at this URL. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
