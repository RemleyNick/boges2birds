Support

Help and contact info for Boges2Birds

Boges2Birds is a structured golf training app. Pick a target — Break 100, 90, or 80 — and the app builds a 4-week practice plan around your weakest skills. Log your rounds and the plan adapts to what you actually need to work on.

Contact

Bug reports, questions, feature requests, or account deletion: remleynick@yahoo.com

You can also send feedback directly from Profile → Send feedback inside the app — it includes basic diagnostic info to help us reproduce issues.

Frequently asked

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed by Apple. Open the Settings app on your iPhone → tap your name → Subscriptions → tap Boges2Birds → Cancel Subscription. You'll keep premium access until the end of the current billing period.

How do I restart my training plan?

Open the Profile tab → tap Reassess skills to update your self-ratings, or Change program to switch between Break 100/90/80. Either action generates a fresh 4-week training block.

How do I delete my account?

Email us at remleynick@yahoo.com with the subject "Delete my account" from the email address tied to your account. We'll remove your account and all associated data within 30 days.

Why didn't my round sync to the cloud?

The app works offline — round logs, drill completions, and skill updates are saved locally first and synced when you're back online and the app is in the foreground. If something seems stuck, force-quit and reopen the app; sync runs on launch.

I'm seeing a crash or a blank screen.

Force-quit and reopen the app first. If it keeps happening, send us a note from Profile → Send feedback — that submission automatically includes the info we need to investigate.
